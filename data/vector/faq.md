# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I track my order?

You can track your order by providing your order number to our support agent.
We'll retrieve the latest shipment status and tracking information from our system.
Tracking updates are available once your order has been shipped.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

## How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days.
Expedited shipping takes 2–3 business days.
Overnight shipping is available for orders placed before 2 PM EST.

## Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement.
After that, please contact support and we will do our best to assist.
Once an order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

## Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship to the US, Canada, UK, and Australia.
International orders may be subject to customs fees.

## How do I contact support?

You can reach our AI support agent 24/7 via this voice interface.
For complex issues, a human agent is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST.

## Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
We never share your information with third parties without consent.
