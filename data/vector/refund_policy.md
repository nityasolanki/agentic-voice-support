# Refund Policy

## Eligibility

You may request a refund within 30 days of the delivery date.
Items must be unused and in their original packaging.
Digital products and downloadable software are non-refundable.

## Process

To initiate a refund, contact our support agent with your order number.
Our agent will verify eligibility and submit the refund request automatically.
You will receive an email confirmation with your refund ID.

## Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
The refund will be returned to your original payment method.
Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

## Non-Refundable Items

- Digital downloads
- Gift cards
- Items marked "Final Sale"
- Personalized or custom-made products

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, we will replace it at no cost.
Contact support within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damage.
A replacement will be shipped within 2 business days of approval.

## Return Shipping

For eligible returns, we provide a prepaid shipping label.
Items must be returned within 14 days of refund approval.
